# Weekly Reports

Weekly company news roundups covering the major AI labs. Published every Monday, covering the preceding Monday through Sunday.

Format:

- **Headline** --- one-line summary of the news item
- **What changed** --- concrete description of the announcement or development
- **Why it matters** --- strategic or technical significance
- **Source** --- link to official announcement, blog, or repository

Companies tracked: DeepSeek, Google DeepMind, Meta, OpenAI, Anthropic, Microsoft.
